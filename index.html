Common Questions

FAQ

Do I need an appointment?
No. We are a walk-in only barbershop. Come in any day of the week during business hours and take a seat. First come, first served.
How much does a haircut cost?
Haircuts start at $10. Pricing varies by service type. Beard trims, hot towel treatments, and combo services are available at competitive rates. Ask in-shop for the full price list.
Do you offer military discounts?
Yes. We proudly serve the military community near Quantico and offer discounts for active duty service members. Bring your military ID.
Do you cut kids' hair?
Absolutely. Our barbers are patient and experienced with children of all ages. We make the experience comfortable and fun for young ones.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash and all major credit cards.
Do you have a loyalty program?
Yes. Ask for a loyalty card on your visit. Collect stamps with each haircut and earn a free cut after a set number of visits.
What are your hours?
We are open 7 days a week. Monday through Friday 9 AM to 7 PM, Saturday 9 AM to 6 PM, and Sunday 9 AM to 7 PM.
Where are you located?
We are at 2773 Richmond Hwy, Suite 101, Stafford, VA 22554. Located on Route 1, easy to find with plenty of parking.
