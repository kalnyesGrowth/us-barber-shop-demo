Our Story

Cutting Hair Since 2008

U.S. Barber Shop has been a staple on Richmond Highway in Stafford for over 18 years. What started as a neighborhood barbershop has grown into one of the most trusted spots for men's haircuts in the area.

Located minutes from Quantico Marine Corps Base, we proudly serve active duty service members, veterans, and the entire Stafford community. We offer military discounts because we appreciate the sacrifice. Every customer gets the same treatment: a quality cut, a hot towel finish, and a complimentary drink while they wait.

We are a women-owned business that takes pride in keeping our shop clean, our prices fair, and our customers looking sharp.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an appointment?

No. We are a walk-in barbershop. Just stop by during business hours and we will get you in the chair. We are open 7 days a week.

Do you offer military discounts?

Yes. We proudly offer discounts for active duty military and veterans. Just bring your military ID. We are located minutes from Quantico Marine Corps Base.

How much is a haircut?

Haircuts start at just $10. We believe in providing quality cuts at fair prices. Shaves and combo services are available at competitive rates too.

What types of cuts do you do?

We do all styles: fades (low, mid, high, skin), classic cuts, military regs, high and tights, scissor cuts, razor cuts, and custom styles. Just tell your barber what you want.

Do you do hot towel shaves?

Yes. We offer traditional hot towel straight razor shaves. Every haircut also finishes with a complimentary hot towel.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept cash and all major credit and debit cards.

Do you have a loyalty program?

Yes. We offer loyalty cards. Get your card stamped with each visit and earn a free cut after a certain number of visits.

Are you open on Sundays?

Yes. We are open 7 days a week. Sunday hours are 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM.
